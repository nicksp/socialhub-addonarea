You can `introduce` new addons by clicking on `Create Addon` button in the top menu section, so that you can see Addons badge number update.

The logic is as follows:

  • When you route to `Addons` page, we reset the number of new, unvisible addons.
  • You can't add new addons (by clicking a button) when being on the `Addons` page. Since you are viewing all the new addons.
